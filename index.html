More on Oscillo ü¶â

Oscillo's official species type is a stilt owl. Yeah, I didn't exactly want it to reflect the Pok√©mon (singular and plural) whom his design was inspired by, but I suppose that still turned out pretty well, all things considered. I often tell people that I picked an owl as a species of choice, simply because it spoke to me - and I really do mean it. On a spiritual level, relation to a job I used to work as and still truly love, averseness to wanting a generic sona species, me being an actual night owl in practice, etc., ... it just felt right. That's all I can really say, I have no real reason to think so otherwise.

I attribute his name to one of my personal traits, unlike what some may think I have a thing for oscilloscopes. I tend to get very, very indecisive.. to a fault at times. Just like that, I imagined me going back and forth on my decision-making process being similar to an oscillation motion. Voil√†, that was it. I also didn't think that naming him "Oscillo the Owl" felt right, hence why I decided to give him a proper last name. At the time I thought "strix" was prevalently used in owls' scientific names; that plus it sounding cool motivated my choice for the last name. I soon learnt "bubo" was used more, but I can definitely say it may not have been picked had I learnt this first.

Overall, I conjured the character to match me in real life. That just means that regardless of if you view me as my actual person self or this hoot as my identity, there's practically no difference. Unlike some others who make their own furry sonas to reflect who they wish to be, I modeled Oscillo after myself so as to remind myself to never lose my own identity as a person - owl or not. And with that I think I've done a good job with that so far!
